Heart Failure Prediction (Using Machine Learning)

  • Cardiovascular disorders (CVDs) cause heart failure, which accounts for 31% of all fatalities worldwide.
  • By entering relevant attributes, an ML Model on a webpage predicted heart failure. We compared classifier accuracy, Classification Report, Confusion Matrix, Precision Recall Curve, and ROC curves.
  • we concluded taht Aged hearts fail. >120 mcg/L creatinine phosphokinase and 55%–70% ejection fraction. below 55%. Platelets ruined hearts. Most cardiac failures occur at 0.8–1.7 mg/dL creatinine. Heart failure risk rises above 130 mEq/L serum sodium. Diabetics and anemics risk heart failure. Hypertension raises heart failure risk. Heart failure risk is slightly higher in men. Smoking causes heart failure.

Study of Ethics for Data Product

  • As part of my study of the ethics used in data product development, I developed a game questionnaire survey to collect data from users while maintaining the 5Cs (consent, clarity, consistency, control, and consequences).
